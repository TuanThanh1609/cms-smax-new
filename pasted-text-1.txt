- Use semantic headings
- Keep form labels visible
- Ensure Vietnamese text remains readable on gradients

--------------------------------------------------
27. MOTION AND MICRO-INTERACTIONS
--------------------------------------------------

Use subtle motion only.

Recommended interactions:

- Slow orbital movement in the hero
- Floating product nodes with 4–8px movement
- Animated connection paths
- Small UI cards appearing sequentially
- Button arrow movement
- Automation stages lighting up while scrolling
- Customer journey progress animation
- Integration logo hover states

Avoid:

- Fast spinning
- Strong parallax
- Flashing neon
- Large cursor-following effects
- Distracting continuous animations
